# 音频设置

## 概述

音频设置由 `InitializeAudioSettings` 函数创建，位于 `LyraGameSettingRegistry_Audio.cpp`。音频设置分为 Volume（音量）和 Sound（声音）两大部分。

## InitializeAudioSettings 结构

```
AudioSettings (UGameSettingCollection)
  ├── DisplayName: "Audio"
  │
  ├── Volume (UGameSettingCollection)
  │     ├── OverallVolume (UGameSettingValueScalarDynamic)
  │     │     └── 数据源: GET_LOCAL_SETTINGS_FUNCTION_PATH(GetOverallVolume)
  │     │     └── 格式: ZeroToOnePercent (0~1 → 0%~100%)
  │     ├── MusicVolume (UGameSettingValueScalarDynamic)
  │     │     └── 数据源: GET_LOCAL_SETTINGS_FUNCTION_PATH(GetMusicVolume)
  │     ├── SoundEffectsVolume (UGameSettingValueScalarDynamic)
  │     │     └── 数据源: GET_LOCAL_SETTINGS_FUNCTION_PATH(GetSoundFXVolume)
  │     ├── DialogueVolume (UGameSettingValueScalarDynamic)
  │     │     └── 数据源: GET_LOCAL_SETTINGS_FUNCTION_PATH(GetDialogueVolume)
  │     └── VoiceChatVolume (UGameSettingValueScalarDynamic)
  │           └── 数据源: GET_LOCAL_SETTINGS_FUNCTION_PATH(GetVoiceChatVolume)
  │
  └── Sound (UGameSettingCollection)
        ├── SubtitlesPage (UGameSettingCollectionPage)
        │     ├── DisplayName: "Subtitles"
        │     ├── SubtitlesEnabled (UGameSettingValueDiscreteDynamic_Bool)
        │     │     └── 数据源: GET_SHARED_SETTINGS_FUNCTION_PATH(GetSubtitlesEnabled)
        │     ├── SubtitlesTextSize (UGameSettingValueDiscreteDynamic_Enum)
        │     │     └── 枚举值: Small / Medium / Large / ExtraLarge
        │     │     └── 数据源: GET_SHARED_SETTINGS_FUNCTION_PATH(GetSubtitlesTextSize)
        │     ├── SubtitlesTextColor (UGameSettingValueDiscreteDynamic_Color)
        │     │     └── 数据源: GET_SHARED_SETTINGS_FUNCTION_PATH(GetSubtitlesTextColor)
        │     ├── SubtitlesTextBorder (UGameSettingValueDiscreteDynamic_Enum)
        │     │     └── 枚举值: None / Outline / DropShadow
        │     │     └── 数据源: GET_SHARED_SETTINGS_FUNCTION_PATH(GetSubtitlesTextBorder)
        │     └── SubtitlesBackgroundOpacity (UGameSettingValueDiscreteDynamic_Enum)
        │           └── 枚举值: 0%/25%/50%/75%/100%
        │           └── 数据源: GET_SHARED_SETTINGS_FUNCTION_PATH(GetSubtitlesBackgroundOpacity)
        ├── AudioOutputDevice (ULyraSettingValueDiscreteDynamic_AudioOutputDevice)
        │     └── 平台条件: TAG_Platform_Trait_SupportsChangingAudioOutputDevice
        │     └── 数据源: GET_LOCAL_SETTINGS_FUNCTION_PATH(GetAudioOutputDeviceId)
        ├── BackgroundAudio (UGameSettingValueDiscreteDynamic_Enum)
        │     └── 平台条件: TAG_Platform_Trait_SupportsBackgroundAudio
        │     └── 数据源: GET_SHARED_SETTINGS_FUNCTION_PATH(GetAllowAudioInBackgroundSetting)
        ├── HeadphoneMode (UGameSettingValueDiscreteDynamic_Enum)
        │     └── 数据源: GET_LOCAL_SETTINGS_FUNCTION_PATH(GetHeadphoneMode)
        │     └── 自定义条件: CanModifyHeadphoneModeEnabled() lambda
        └── HDRAudio (UGameSettingValueDiscreteDynamic_Bool)
              └── 数据源: GET_LOCAL_SETTINGS_FUNCTION_PATH(GetHDRAudioModeEnabled)
```

## 数据源模式

音量设置全部使用 `GET_LOCAL_SETTINGS_FUNCTION_PATH` 绑定到 `ULyraSettingsLocal` 的属性。字幕设置使用 `GET_SHARED_SETTINGS_FUNCTION_PATH` 绑定到 `ULyraSettingsShared` 的属性。

## 平台特性控制

| 设置 | 平台标签 | 行为 |
|------|---------|------|
| 音频输出设备 | `TAG_Platform_Trait_SupportsChangingAudioOutputDevice` | 缺失时 Kill |
| 背景音频 | `TAG_Platform_Trait_SupportsBackgroundAudio` | 缺失时 Kill |
| 耳机模式 | 自定义 lambda `CanModifyHeadphoneModeEnabled()` | 按运行时条件禁用 |
